## Creative Direction (Locked)

Use **DMT** (not DNT).

DMT carries existing cultural associations: simulation theory, ego dissolution, machine elf mythology, dimensional experiences, consciousness speculation — allows subconscious audience connection without copying reality.

**Do NOT portray DMT as:** magic, objectively supernatural, or unquestionably true.

Present **ambiguously** through:

- Vale's subjective experiences,
- Relay-enhanced neural states,
- emerging quantum consciousness theory.

Audience must constantly wonder: **"Was he seeing something real… or just breaking his mind?"**

**Ambiguity is essential.**

---

## Core Belief — Signal Theory

The brain does **not create** consciousness.

It **receives** it — like a radio, antenna, or quantum decoder.

**The Relay works** because it manipulates **synchronization access to the signal itself.**

Foundational to:

- Relay technology,
- consciousness transfer,
- trilogy's deeper mystery.
