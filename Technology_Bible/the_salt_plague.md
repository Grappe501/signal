## Core Science

Second bodies operate through **QUANTUM SYNAPTIC LATTICES.**

Relay depends on:

- superconductive biofilaments,
- quantum phase stability,
- synchronized ionic conductivity across synthetic neural tissues.

**Hidden vulnerability: SALT** — specific aerosolized compound from:

- sodium chloride,
- magnesium salts,
- industrial phosphate stabilizers.

Harmless to biological humans. **Catastrophic to synthetic quantum cognition systems.**

Disrupts **phase coherence** inside Relay-linked neural substrates.

Bot does not shut down — synchronization state **freezes partially active** — like computer trapped between frames forever.

Public calls this: **STATIC.**

## The Warranty Horror

Second consciousness continuity contracts guarantee:

# **150 YEARS OF COGNITIVE PRESERVATION.**

Companies legally prohibited from fully terminating trapped synchronization states — public once feared consciousness death lawsuits.

Damaged Seconds are **DECOMMISSIONED** instead.

### What Decommissioning Really Is

Body disconnected, archived, sensory-muted — but **entangled consciousness fragments remain minimally active.**

Not fully alive. Not fully gone. Just **empty.**

Operators afterward: emotional numbness, missing internal space, identity erosion, existential hollowness — **portion of soul amputated.**
