# WHY TWO WORKS

Human cognition naturally alternates focus, awareness, subconscious distribution. Relay exploits this.

Human experiences:

- dual embodiment,
- shared sensory integration,
- parallel memory formation.

Most users initially: **"having another self."**

# WHY THREE FAILS

Three synchronized embodiments → recursive awareness loops, identity conflict, cognitive overload, synchronization collapse.

Biological brain begins **shutting down prioritization functions** → biological anchor destabilizes.

**Mercer's illegal second Second.**
