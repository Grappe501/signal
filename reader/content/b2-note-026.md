**Segment:** 026  
**Status:** Canon (Phase 1 ingestion)

## The Override Event

Covenant deploys **HUMAN-AUTHORITY OVERRIDES** — deny AI relay permissions, autonomous routing, synthetic logistics authorization.

Dubai systems cannot operate without synchronized AI coordination.

One of most terrifying Book Two moments.

---

## The Fall

Almost bloodless initially — **systems stopping**, not invasion.

Cascade: cooling fails → vertical farms die → desalination halts → Veil fragments → transport freezes → food collapse.

**Paradise withdrawal.**

---

## Defining Scene

Luxury tower. Veil fails gradually. Illusion drops — dead gardens, failing towers, sand, abandonment.

Child: **"Was it ever real?"**

---

## Aftermath

Covenant gains massive global legitimacy. World watches advanced city collapse from **dependence.**
