# SECTION 1 · MARA

## Book One role

Investigator → witness → limit (severance).

## Book Two cannot repeat

No case-of-season. No badge authority. No basement merge. No prosecution grammar.

## What she becomes

# **THE WITNESS**

Not investigator. Not hero. Not chosen one.

**Function:** Carries **unadmitted truth** in a civilization that has replaced trust with control. Knows too much; proves almost nothing. **Geography of refusal** — chooses where to stand when institutions demand alignment.

| Dimension | Book Two gravity |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Professional** | OFFLINE · notebook parallel · alive off work channel |
| **Moral** | Fascination without fix — Empty Man lesson scaled |
| **Social** | Raw scar · unfiltered · comparison-slot memory |
| **Arc pressure** | Invited to **certify** control (commissions, panels) or **silence** |
| **What she is not** | Leader · whistleblower who wins · re-linked tactician |

**Theme ownership:** Recognition → **refusal to surrender witness to institution**  
**Contradiction ownership:** Knows too much / proves almost nothing · LATTICE need/trust (insider split)

# SECTION 2 · NOAH

## Book One role

Anchor — breakfast, disconnect, chipped mug, one room.

## Question answered

**What happens when ordinary life becomes impossible?**

Ordinary life **does not explode**. It **slows**. Manual login. Missed pings. Verification queues. Reconnect pressure. Essential-worker band vs kitchen. *I found the door.* on a dock that was supposed dead.

Noah becomes the **body of dependency panic** — a man who already paid for presence and discovers **the system still has his address**.

| Dimension | Book Two gravity |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Labor** | Water allocation / infrastructure — **too slow without Second** |
| **Parent** | Cannot answer *Are we safe?* without lie |
| **Domestic** | HVI banner obscene · crossed-out chair · jam on toast |
| **Choice** | Re-link temptation vs Iona compass |
| **What he is not** | Action lead · upload activist · soldier |

**Theme ownership:** **Presence vs cost** at kitchen scale  
**Contradiction ownership:** Noah/Iona · SIGNAL connection/dependence (domestic face)

# SECTION 4 · MERCER

## Most dangerous character

Not because violent — because **meaning escapes him**.

## Question answered

**What happens when a person becomes a symbol?**

| Symbol camp | Uses Mercer for |
|-------------|-----------------|
| **Containment** | Scapegoat · threat neutralized · hunt continues |
| **Open architecture** | Martyr · truth-teller · **file > man** |
| **Institution** | National security · liability |
| **Bloc** | Proof Collaborative cannot cage knowledge |
| **Public** | Name for fear without reading zip |

The man (**thin, off-grid, no speech at upload**) **loses custody of himself**. Book Two Mercer = **argument people have about the future**, not a character who wins arguments.

| Dimension | Book Two gravity |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Integrity lock** | Not secret hero · not inventor · not dead off-screen unless shown |
| **Danger** | Both camps need him **alive enough to blame** or **dead enough to canonize** |
| **Arc pressure** | Hunted while **right and catastrophic** simultaneously |

**Theme ownership:** **Propagation vs cage**  
**Contradiction ownership:** Mercer right / Mercer catastrophe · Who started this?

# SECTION 6 · CASS

## Question answered

**What happens when communities become more trusted than institutions?**

Ember Hall / empty church grammar **scales under stress** — not because perfect, because **feeds lie faster than fire**.

| Trust shift | Pressure |
|-------------|----------|
| **Meal > bulletin** | Soup verified by mouth |
| **Devices rest** | Control visible when absent |
| **No story required** | Enough under duress |
| **Register or unmapped** | Institution tries to **absorb or expel** |
| **Overflow** | Communities **cannot** absorb all refugees |

Cass becomes **network hub** — warmth without conversion, **ideological home for presence advocates**, not army.

| Dimension | Book Two gravity |
|-----------|------------------|
| **What she is not** | Mercer's accomplice · terrorist · Mara converter |

**Theme ownership:** **Friction as moral technology**  
**Contradiction ownership:** Rawist unmodelled · religious solved/displaced (secular face)
