## Creative & Ethical Principle

The company gains depth by being **sincere rather than evil**.

The tragedy is **systemic emergence**, not cartoon villainy.

- visionary founders,
- genuinely humanitarian goals,
- open-source idealism,
- democratized AI access,
- anti-authoritarian philosophy,
- and unintended civilization-scale consequences.

---

## Design Goals — Company Name

Should feel:

- modern,
- optimistic,
- socially connective,
- slightly philosophical,
- soft-tech rather than militaristic,
- and believable as a real trillion-dollar company.

Should sound like: connection, networks, humanity, layers, consciousness, or shared experience.

**Avoid:** evil-sounding names, cyberpunk clichés, harsh sci-fi branding, obvious Meta copies.

**Audience should initially trust this company completely.**

## Canonical Company Name

# LATTICE

**Top recommendation — author confirmed in Segment 007.**

### Meaning

A structure of interconnected points.

### Feels

Elegant, tech-forward, philosophical, scalable.

### Tagline Possibilities

- "Closer Together."
- "Shared Human Experience."
- "Connection Without Friction."

### Why It Works

A lattice is invisible infrastructure holding society together.

By Book Three, the irony becomes powerful:
**humanity became trapped inside the lattice.**

|-------|---------|

| Neural lattice | Brain networking |
| Reality lattice | The Veil |
| Power lattice | Civilization infrastructure |

Clean. Prestigious. Not evil.
