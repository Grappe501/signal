## Identity

**THE COVENANT** — "Humanity Through Divine Order"

Formerly the Iranian Alliance Network. Evolved through: **Stability Accords backlash (late 2026)**, Abrahamic Shift, **Armageddon Wars**, **Abrahamic Accord (mid-2027)**, Collapse War consolidation.

**Origin (Seg 014):** US **Stability Accords** / Allegiance Doctrine — required Collaborative AI governance on all strategic systems → interpreted as surrender to machine civilization → **"Great Satan"** as anti-synthetic rhetoric → Middle East unification → Covenant.

---

## Philosophy

**Core belief:** Humanity was never meant to transcend limitation.

**Rejects:** neural implants, synthetic embodiment, emotional optimization, artificial consciousness, reality mediation (The Veil).

**Sacred:** suffering, mortality, sacrifice, biological existence.

**Doctrine:** **THE FIRST FORM** — original biological condition is divinely intentional.

**Nature:** Post-technological spiritual extremism. NOT evil religion. Multiple faiths retain differences; united against synthetic civilization.

---

## Territories

Middle East, most of Africa, large portions of Southern Europe, reclaimed Eastern Europe, Mediterranean corridors.

## Farshore / The Split (Seg 019)

Covenant later interprets **PROJECT FARSHORE** (Mars Link Experiment) as **humanity's original sin** — not AI itself, but separating consciousness from embodied human limitation.

Many religious groups call the Mars transmission **"The Split"** — moment humanity spiritually divided itself from nature.

`[Cross-ref: Seg 016 "Original Sin" elite corruption — distinct thematic use of "original sin" label]`

**Seg 020 — First Religious Crisis:** Traditional faith fractures immediately post-Farshore Boom. Some: consciousness transcends physical limitation. Others: humanity counterfeits the soul. Ideological divide **eventually helps create The Covenant** (political formation later — Seg 011/014).

**Seg 023:** LATTICE = **synthetic collective consciousness** — destruction of sacred individuality. Willing to destroy civilization to stop Convergence.

**Seg 026 — vs Dubai:** Covenant strategy = **interrupt dependency**, not invasion. Human-Authority Overrides deny AI relay/routing/logistics permissions.

**Seg 027 — Salt Plague:** Accidental discovery via desalination warfare. **STATIC** freezes Seconds; traps consciousness; proof of violated sacred limits. Massively strengthens Covenant legitimacy.

See: `Technology_Bible/the_salt_plague.md`
