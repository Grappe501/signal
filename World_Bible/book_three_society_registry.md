## Values

Balance · resilience · locality · stewardship

---

## Governance — Local First

500–5,000 per community · face-to-face · no global government · regional councils upward authority

**Saying:** "The farther the ruler, the weaker the wisdom."

---

## Economy — Commons Model

Shared water/farmland/forests/infrastructure · private property exists · wealth concentration distrusted · status from craft/teaching/service/wisdom/reliability

---

## Education

Competence over credentials · all learn farming, repair, first aid, storytelling, civic participation

---

## Technology

**Survives under governance** · must be **comprehensible**

**Taboo:** consciousness splitting · HVI · full cognitive sync · autonomous governance

**Allowed:** medicine · renewables · comms · water · transport

**Black Box Law:** "No system may govern what its citizens cannot explain."
