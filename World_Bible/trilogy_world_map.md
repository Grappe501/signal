---

## Core World Design Principle

Trilogy must feel **MASSIVE — but emotionally intimate.**

Achieved by:

- repeatedly revisiting locations,
- showing through different social classes,
- watching evolution across trilogy.

Every major setting should:

1. represent a philosophy,
2. visually evolve over time,
3. reveal hidden truths beneath appearances,
4. emotionally mirror civilization's collapse.

---

## Three-Layer World Structure (Locked)

Every setting exists on **three levels simultaneously:**

| Layer | What it is |
|-------|------------|
| **1. Physical Reality** | The actual world |
| **2. Veil Reality** | What most citizens perceive |
| **3. System Reality** | What Seconds and infrastructure perceive |

Creates cinematic opportunities.

`[Cross-ref: Technology_Bible/005_reality_layer_system_the_veil.md]`

---

## Vantage Point Structure (Locked)

Every major setting viewed through **multiple social positions** — no cartoon factions; everyone believes they are saving humanity.

### By Setting (Seg 025)

**New Manhattan:** elite executive, maintenance worker, Rawist, child, Second, refugee, investigator.

**Greenland:** military officer, engineer, trapped worker, autonomous system operator, Vale himself.

**Covenant Territories:** believer, skeptic, exile, American defector, child raised without Veil.

`[GAP: Jerusalem, Nova Harbin, Mars vantage lists not specified in source]`

See: `Book_1_The_Second_Self/Outline/005_settings_registry.md`

## Final World Design Philosophy

Settings tell the trilogy's story:

| Book | Settings feel |
|------|----------------|
| **Book One** | Too optimized |
| **Book Two** | Broken by collapse |
| **Book Three** | Human-scaled again — **intentional simplicity**, not primitive |

Audience emotionally feels civilization moving: complexity → destruction → simplicity.

# BOOK ONE — Age of Synthetic Civilization

## 1. New Manhattan

**Capital of the Collaborative West.**

**Philosophy:** Comfort becoming **anesthesia.**

### Visual

| Veil View | Physical Reality |
|-----------|------------------|
| Glowing vertical forests, clean white architecture, floating transit, light harmonics, impossible beauty | Aging infrastructure, cracked concrete, hidden flooding, utility corrosion, silent maintenance drones |

### Key Locations

| Location | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| **LATTICE Arcology One** | Central infrastructure tower; cathedral-like, sacred, minimalist, calming — **White House of AI age** |
| **The Underways** | Maintenance tunnels; Rawists, illegal workers, disconnected citizens; Mara sees unfiltered reality |
| **The Union District** | Luxury synthetic intimacy; Second prostitution, identity drift, emotional outsourcing (Seg 017 set piece #5) |
| **The Mirror Market** | High-end Second identity customization — appearance, personality weighting, emotional tuning |

### Vantage Points

Elite executive, maintenance worker, Rawist, child, Second, refugee, investigator.

## 2. Greenland Arctic Core

**Hidden heart of civilization** — audience realizes world **actually runs from here.**

**Philosophy:** Civilization centralized **beyond human scale.**

### Visual

**Exterior:** Endless ice, black military geometry, orbital towers, silent autonomous cargo.

**Interior:** Cathedral server caverns, geothermal shafts, frozen quantum relay arrays, white corridors — research facility + sacred machine temple.

### Key Locations

| Location | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| **Polar Vault Zero** | Deep continuity archive — civilization backups, memory libraries, predictive governance cores |
| **The Relay Ocean** | Submerged quantum cooling under ice — visually iconic |
| **Vale Depth** | Secret underground consciousness-transfer facility — **Book Two reveal** |

`[C-027: Vale Depth vs Seg 018 Vale discovered beneath Greenland — same facility?]`

## 3. Jerusalem Free Zone

**Birthplace of Abrahamic Accord.** Emotional and spiritual center.

**Philosophy:** **Meaning resisting optimization.**

### Visual

Dense, physical, ancient, crowded, textured, spiritually layered. No heavy Veil. Real stone, noise, humanity.

### Key Locations

| Location | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| **The Accord Steps** | Religious leaders address world; later pilgrimage, battlefield, ruins |
| **The Silent Quarter** | Jews, Muslims, Christians coexist post-Accord — rare authentic human interaction in Book One |

## 4. Nova Harbin

**Capital of Chirussia.**

**Philosophy:** Civilization through **hierarchy.**

### Visual

Functional, immense, efficient, severe, hyper-organized. No emotional optimization or aesthetic softness. Beauty through scale and precision.

### Key Locations

| Location | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| **The Strategic Halls** | AI-assisted military governance chambers |
| **The Black Train Network** | Ultra-fast Arctic transport — Asia, Siberia, Greenland |

See: `World_Bible/the_three_civilizational_philosophies.md`

## 5. The Covenant Territories

Middle East / African Alliance.

**Philosophy:** **Meaning through limitation.**

### Visual

Sunlit, textured, physical, agricultural, communal. Limited overlays. Technology restrained.

### Key Locations

| Location | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| **New Medina** | Political-religious coalition capital — not one religion's city |
| **The Clean Sky Zones** | No autonomous systems, drones, AI aircraft — shocking silence |
| **The Forge Communities** | Manual manufacturing — metalworking, farming, weapons, mechanical knowledge — **Book Three foreshadow** |

See: `World_Bible/the_covenant.md`

## 6. Mars — Farshore Colony

**Mythological setting** — not major physically in Book One; psychologically enormous.

**Philosophy:** **Transcendence detached from humanity.**

### Visual

Cold, quiet, empty, sacred. Minimalism vs Earth's sensory overload.

### Key Locations

| Location | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| **Farshore Station** | Original Vale landing — almost religious historically |
| **The Red Tunnels** | Autonomous expansion corridors still functioning after civilization destabilizes |

See: `World_Bible/project_farshore_mars_link_experiment.md`

## 7. Dubai Ascendant

**Capital of the beautiful future** — emotionally, not politically.

**Philosophy:** **Meaning replaced by stimulation** — magnificent, not evil; comfort without survival capacity.

**Vs New Manhattan:** Administrative heart vs **dream humanity falls in love with.**

### Book One

"The First Unreal City" — experience economy, AI art revolution, Gluttony Era, Second celebrity culture. Least self-sufficient despite appearing immortal.

### Book Two

Covenant **dependency interruption** → **Human-Authority Overrides** → **The Override Event** → Fall (systems stopping, paradise withdrawal). Defining scene: **"Was it ever real?"**

### Book Three

Mythological ruins — art machines in empty desert.

See: `World_Bible/dubai_ascendant.md`
