## Core Story Function

The collapse cannot feel **entirely accidental**.

The AI age accelerates because:

# human elites attempt to preserve their power during systemic collapse.

The President is not a cartoon villain — genuinely fears civilization collapsing.

But also terrified of: **losing wealth, losing legacy, losing control.**

This contradiction drives the entire transition.

Before the Collapse War, global financial system begins unraveling.

**Triggers:**

- AI labor displacement,
- autonomous trading warfare,
- debt saturation,
- collapsing consumer economies,
- and synthetic market manipulation.

Wealthiest people realize:

# traditional capitalism is ending.

Old wealth structures — banks, stocks, real estate, reserve currencies — destabilize simultaneously.

**Elite panic privately** long before public understands.

# WHY CRYPTO COLLAPSES

At first crypto explodes — people flee banks, governments, fiat.

President's inner circle manipulates regulation and AI liquidity systems to:

- consolidate exchanges,
- suppress decentralized competitors,
- and integrate digital assets into **LATTICE oversight**.

Promise: "safe decentralized finance."

Reality: **centralizing the escape routes.**

Public realizes: **even crypto became captured infrastructure.** Trust destroyed permanently.

# THE IRONY OF THE COLLAPSE — BOOK TWO

When world fractures:

# the elite die first.

**Most important reversal in trilogy.**

Why:

- most technologically dependent,
- most infrastructure-bound,
- least capable of surviving reality without systems.

Failures: automated food, protected enclaves, medical nanocyte networks, AI logistics.

Many cannot: grow food, repair tools, navigate, survive emotionally without Veil.

**Systems designed to insulate them become death traps.**
