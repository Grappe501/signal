# The Retinal Authority Act

**Segment:** 002  
**Status:** Canon (Phase 1 ingestion)

---

## The Most Important Law on Earth

**THE RETINAL AUTHORITY ACT**

Humans can only legally interact with society through:

# LIVE RETINAL VERIFICATION

Every human has a registered biological retinal signature impossible for bots to replicate in real time.

This becomes the **foundation of civilization**.

---

## Required For

Retinal verification is needed to:

- access money,
- enter buildings,
- travel,
- vote,
- own property,
- buy food,
- activate medical care,
- authenticate communication,
- deactivate your Second,
- marry,
- have children,
- access education,
- or use transportation.

---

## The Irony

Humans are technically **"more free" than bots** — but only if they **continuously prove they are human**.
