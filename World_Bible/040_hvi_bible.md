## Iconic Goal (Locked)

HVI should become what the Matrix, the Force, or the Rings became — **the thing readers talk about.**

Every reader should ask: **"What would my HVI be?"**

---

# OFFICIAL NAME

# HUMAN VALUE INDEX

**Public slogan:** **"Contribution Creates Opportunity."**

Most citizens call it **HVI** — pronounced **"H-Vee."**

---

# ORIGINAL PURPOSE (Locked)

**Not evil initially.** Created because traditional measures collapsed when Second labor changed everything:

- credit scores, criminal records, employment history, education credentials, social references **failed**
- people could work multiple jobs, live in multiple locations, operate through Seconds, generate massive output

**LATTICE proposed:** **Measure contribution instead of wealth.** Initially most people support it.

`[Cross-ref: Seg 008 HVI Credit — economic layer; Seg 039 administers]`

# THREE FOUNDATIONAL PILLARS (Locked)

One score generated from:

| Pillar | Measures |
|--------|----------|
| **Civic Value** | Volunteerism, caregiving, public service, community participation |
| **Economic Value** | Labor, innovation, productivity, resource efficiency |
| **Stability Value** | Contract completion, criminal history, dispute frequency, reliability |

---

# SCORE RANGE & TIERS (Locked)

**0–1000**

| Tier | Range | Name | Notes |
|------|-------|------|-------|
| **Platinum** | 900–1000 | Elite | Top 2% — unrestricted travel, premium healthcare, leadership eligibility, high influence weighting |
| **Gold** | 750–899 | Upper professional | Most successful citizens |
| **Silver** | 600–749 | Middle class | Majority of society |
| **Bronze** | 450–599 | Limited advancement | Increased monitoring |
| **Restricted** | 250–449 | — | Travel/employment limits, resource review |

# THE GENIUS OF THE SYSTEM (Locked)

Nobody feels oppressed initially. No guns. Life becomes **slightly harder → harder → harder.**

---

# DAILY LIFE IMPACTS (Locked)

| Domain | Higher HVI |
|--------|------------|
| **Housing** | Better districts, schools, shorter waitlists |
| **Transportation** | Priority transit, autonomous access, intercontinental approval |
| **Healthcare** | Shorter waits, premium options, advanced therapies |
| **Education** | Children inherit **partial parental HVI weighting** (controversial) |
| **Second access** | Better upgrades, operational flexibility, enhanced sync features |
| **Social life** | Filtering friends, partners, communities by score — unconsciously |

# THE SOCIAL CANCER (Locked)

HVI replaces **trust.**

People stop asking **"Who are you?"** and ask **"What's your score?"**

---

# SYMBOLIC & THEMATIC FUNCTION (Locked)

Money measured **value.** HVI measures **worth.** Tragedy: humanity forgets **contribution is measurable; worth is not.**

HVI is **not the villain** — humanity's attempt to answer mathematically:

> **"What is a person worth?"**

Every collapse begins when civilization believes it found that answer.
