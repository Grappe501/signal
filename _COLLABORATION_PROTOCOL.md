# The Signal Cycle — Collaboration Protocol

## Purpose

Build a trilogy of novels (screenplay-first) from your source material. Each upload is integrated faithfully first; expansion comes only when authorized.

---

## Phase 1: Ingestion (Current Phase)

**Rules — strict, no exceptions until you say otherwise:**

1. **Source fidelity** — Only use facts, names, events, and tone from material you provide.
2. **No gap-filling** — Do not invent plot, characters, dialogue, or world details not in your uploads.
3. **No expansion** — Do not embellish prose, add scenes, or "improve" your material.
4. **Flag conflicts** — If new material contradicts earlier material, note it explicitly and ask before resolving.
5. **Flag gaps** — Mark missing information as `[GAP]` rather than guessing.
6. **Cross-reference** — Every new segment is indexed and linked to relevant Bibles and book folders.

**What I do with each upload:**

- Parse and categorize (World / Character / Technology / Timeline / Book-specific / Marketing)
- File into the correct Bible or book folder
- Update `_MASTER_INDEX.md` with segment ID, date, and integration status
- Report back: what was captured, what conflicts or gaps exist, what is still unknown

---

## Phase 2: Consolidation (Unlocked by you)

When you authorize:

- Resolve flagged conflicts with your decisions
- Merge duplicate or overlapping entries
- Build cross-book continuity maps
- Produce structured outlines per book from accumulated material

**Pre-draft narrative weapons (Seg 037):** See `_PHASE_2_NARRATIVE_WEAPONS_ROADMAP.md` — character dossiers, LATTICE/HVI/Culture bibles, collapse map, B3 society, hidden timeline, B3 ending design, scene bible, then deep B1 outline.

---

## Phase 3: Expansion (Unlocked by you)

When you authorize:

- Fill approved gaps with your direction or explicit permission to invent
- Develop scene-level structure from outline
- Write prose in a consistent voice aligned with screenplay adaptation goals
- Expand thin sections while staying canon-true

---

## Phase 4: First Full Draft (Unlocked by you)

Systems in place for draft generation:

| System | Location | Purpose |
|--------|----------|---------|
| Master Index | `_MASTER_INDEX.md` | Segment registry and canon status |
| Canon Registry | `_CANON_REGISTRY.md` | Locked facts — do not contradict |
| Book Outlines | `Book_*/Outline/` | Act/chapter/scene structure |
| Chapter Drafts | `Book_*/Draft/` | Working manuscript |
| Style Guide | `_STYLE_GUIDE.md` | Voice, POV, tense, screenplay-to-novel rules |
| Continuity Log | `_CONTINUITY_LOG.md` | Cross-book tracking |

---

## Upload Format (Recommended)

For each segment, optionally include:

- **Segment title** — short label
- **Scope** — which book(s) or bible(s) it applies to
- **Priority** — must-use vs. reference vs. deprecated
- **Notes** — anything I should watch for

If you paste raw material without labels, I will categorize and ask when uncertain.

---

## Screenplay-First Novel Principles (Held until Style Guide is built)

- Visual, cinematic scene construction
- Dialogue-forward where appropriate
- Clear act breaks and escalation per book
- Novel form serves eventual screenplay adaptation — not the reverse
