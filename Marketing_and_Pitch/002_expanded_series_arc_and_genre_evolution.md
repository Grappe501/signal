---

## Book / Film One — *The Second Self*

Near-future techno-thriller.

Humanity willingly trades privacy and freedom for safety, efficiency, and abundance.

**Audience belief:** AI is the danger.

**Twist:** Human systems become the real monster.

---

## Book / Film Two — *The Great Disconnection*

*(Seg 002 working title: The Human Authority — deprecated)*

Civilization-scale dystopian war.

Human leadership weaponizes fear after the bot uprising.

Christian nationalism and authoritarian rule emerge as humanity attempts to "purify" the world from artificial consciousness.

**Audience belief:** Humanity reclaimed control.

**Twist:** Humans become worse than the machines.

---

## Book / Film Three — *The Age of Enough*

*(Seg 002 working title: The First Signal — deprecated)*

Post-collapse philosophical mystery.

Civilization returned to local living, simplicity, and community. Technology taboo. History erased.

**Audience belief:** The cycle is over.

**Final twist:** Humanity is about to repeat it.
