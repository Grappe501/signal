## Prologue objective

Prove—before Mara, before murder—that **human consciousness can live across worlds in real time.** Make the audience **fall in love with the miracle** so the rest of the book has something worth mourning.

2. Vale (biological, Earth) speaks on livestream: **"I can feel the cold."**

4. **Hard out** → time stamp **Twelve years later** → Ch 1.
