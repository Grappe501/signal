## Job of Act III

Book One stops being a murder mystery and becomes **civilizational origin story** on-page.

| Reader belief entering | Reader belief exiting |
|------------------------|----------------------|
| "How bad is Mercer?" | "The system was always beyond saving in this form." |
| "Mara will solve it" | "Mara can only choose **herself**." |
| "Dubai / Salt / Still Ones are symptoms" | "Awareness without exit is the horror." |
| "Villain will be unmasked" | "There is no villain — only **scale** and **dependency**." |

**Act III does not save the world.** It **reveals** why the world cannot be saved without becoming something else.
