# 1. NARRATIVE FORMAT (Most Important Next Decision)

**Question:** How does the audience experience the world?

| Option | Description | Best for | Weakness |
|--------|-------------|----------|----------|
| **A — Multi-POV Cinematic Thriller** | Rotate Mara, Mercer, Vale, Noah, Covenant, Chirussian officers, civilians, children, Cael lineage seeds | World scale, political depth, nonlinear reveals, trilogy expansion | — |
| **B — Single POV Investigation** | Everything through Mara | Suspense, intimacy, mystery, noir pacing | Harder to convey civilization-scale transformation |
| **C — Limited Rotating POV** | Mainly Mara, Noah, Mercer, Vale fragments, occasional historical/intercept chapters | Intimacy + scale + emotional cohesion | — |

**Author recommendation:** **Option C — Limited Rotating POV**
