## Setup

Mara temporarily **loses access to the Veil** during a **city blackout**.

For the first time, she sees the **real city**.

---

## What She Sees

Not sleek. Not beautiful. Not utopian.

- cracked concrete,
- abandoned districts,
- dead vertical gardens,
- corroded infrastructure,
- graffiti over old Collaborative propaganda,
- collapsed public spaces,
- thousands of people sitting silently indoors wearing **neural immersion halos**.

---

## Narrative Function

The audience realizes:

**The utopia died years ago.**

Only the illusion remained alive.
