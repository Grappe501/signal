## Core Decision (Locked)

# LIMITED ROTATING POV

Not full Game of Thrones. Not single protagonist.

Small number of characters carry narrative; others provide **strategic windows** into the world.

Maximizes: suspense, emotional investment, world scale, mystery, trilogy expansion.

## The Golden Rule (Locked)

Audience should always know:

# slightly less than they think they know.

Every POV must:

- reveal something,
- hide something,
- misinterpret something.

**Nobody sees the entire truth.**

---

# PRIMARY POVs (~85% of Book One)

## POV #1 — MARA VOSS (~45%)

**Role:** The Investigator — **the reader's eyes.**

| Phase | Knowledge |
|-------|-----------|
| Early | Almost nothing |
| Middle | Enough to become dangerous |
| Late | Enough to realize nobody understands full picture |

Provides: mystery, investigation, discovery, emotional grounding.

**Why it works:** Readers learn world naturally because Mara is uncovering it.

**Never through Mara:** private Mercer scenes, private Vale scenes, deep LATTICE operations.

---

## POV #2 — NOAH VALE (~25%)

**Role:** The Ordinary Human.

Answers: **"What is this civilization actually like to live in?"**

Provides: family life, schools, social scoring, daily reality, emotional consequences.

**Essential:** Without Noah, world becomes abstract. Birthdays, parenting, work, relationships, ordinary fears.

**Rule:** Most major world events should first affect ordinary people **through Noah.**

---

## POV #3 — ELI MERCER (~15%)

**Role:** The Rebel — **philosophical accelerant.**

Introduces: forbidden ideas, consciousness questions, anti-system thinking, unpredictability.

**Rule:** Appear dangerous; readers gradually realize he may not be wrong.

**Strategy:** Never reveal full plans, collaborators, true motives until late. Chapters feel **slightly unsettling.**

---

# SECONDARY POVs (Sparingly)

## POV #4 — ADRIAN VALE (~5%)

**Archive fragments only — never normal chapters.**

Formats: interviews, documentaries, speeches, journals, recordings, relay logs.

**Why:** Audience builds mythology around him. Empty Man reveal lands on someone already legendary.
