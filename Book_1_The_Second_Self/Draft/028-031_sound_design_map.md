## Ch 29 — Four-layer sound map

### Layer 1 — Infrastructure
- HVAC groan (vent grilles)
- Maintenance drone buzz + spark
- Construction hammer / jackhammer (3 blocks east)

### Layer 2 — Civic
- Sirens (edges, unmixed)
- Horns (no soft landing)
- PA: **DELAY · MANUAL CHECK · STAND CLEAR**
- Warning klaxon (too long, embarrassed cut)

### Layer 3 — Bodies
- Footsteps (unsoftened cadence)
- Throat clear · sneeze · **cough** (open window)
- Cup plastic clatter
- Couple argument (unmodulated)

### Layer 4 — Absence
- Optimization hum **negative space**
- Proof system was doing unnoticed work

### Human peak
- Child laugh — **one** beat · chest loosen · not stacked superlatives
