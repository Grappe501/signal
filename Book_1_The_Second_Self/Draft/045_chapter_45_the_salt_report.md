The classified terminal did not apologize for existing.

Month Four, week sixteen. CIB secure intake—three levels below the polished floors where briefings sold weather and LATTICE dashboards pretended green was the same thing as safe. Gray room. Honest air. Red classification band on the fragment viewer: **CONTINUITY INTERNAL · FRAGMENT · NEED-TO-KNOW · NOT FOR DISTRIBUTION.** The grammar Mara had learned to read since Month Two—partial on purpose, authority implied, liability deferred.

She badged in with paper copy because some permissions still respected paper the way courts respected signatures. Two signatures. One witness line. The kind of access that arrived in a folder thick enough to bruise a conscience if you read it before coffee. **8841-A** still breathed in a side tab she had not opened on this terminal—not convergence, not yet, not the bar Month Five would eventually hold without drama. **8853-M** still passed verification while failing the world. Harbor Still One still lived in her notebook as **eyes track · public · hold** and in her phone as waveform captures the institution called isolated evaluation. Week sixteen. Filter still off. Raw had become habit. Her eyes worked harder. Her shoulders did not drop.

The escort did not speak. Gray corridor. Gray door. The secure room smelled like filtered air and old carpet and the particular silence institutions bought when they wanted thinking to feel classified instead of moral.

The fragment loaded—not a full report, not a vault, not the mechanism Month Five would eventually name in a forensic suite that used to worry about homicide timelines. A **slice**: cross-site infrastructure summary, sync telemetry annex, maintenance failures listed in bullet grammar too dry to scream. The viewer header pulsed once—**FRAGMENT · PARTIAL · RECOVERY IN PROGRESS**—as if partial were a kindness instead of a liability shield.

Dubai first.

**RELAY SPINE · LOAD OSCILLATION · HANDOFF DELAY · COOLING THRESHOLD BREACH (NON-PUBLIC).**  
**SPECTACLE LAYER · SCHEDULED BEAUTY · MAINTENANCE ACTIVE**—footer disclaimer unchanged while substrate lines underneath did not match nominal.  
**SYNC · NOMINAL** in public font. **SYNC · INSTABILITY FLAG** in the column the public font didn't show.

Not collapse. Not Ch 58 yet. **Listed**—infra failures as rows in a table, the way dependency symptoms arrived in files before they arrived in streets. Mara scrolled without turning her notebook page—time stamp, site code, field name, recovery type. Habit from LATTICE rollups applied to classified grammar: treat the system like a witness that lied politely.

Row three: **COOLING LOOP · DELAY ACKNOWLEDGED · PUBLIC STATUS UNCHANGED.**  
Row five: **HANDOFF QUEUE · BACKLOG · NON-CRITICAL** in the column that meant *critical if you lived downstream.*  
Row eight: **BEAUTY LAYER · NOMINAL · SCHEDULE MAINTAINED** while the relay spine beside it carried oscillation markers like a second heartbeat the spectacle layer refused to subtitle.

The cross-reference engine waited at the margin—a button labeled **COMPARE** that did not promise truth, only adjacency. Mara clicked because adjacency was what investigators had when institutions refused verbs.

Harbor Stack on the next pane.

**PUBLIC TIER · SECOND HOLD · EVALUATION COMPLETE** in the spin column. **SYNC PATHWAY · ANOMALY · CROSS-REF RECOMMENDED** in the column beneath. The cross-reference engine had done what cross-reference engines did when humans refused to say *same family* aloud—it highlighted without concluding.

Sync instability.

Still One family hint—not mechanism, not biochemistry, not the word *awareness* in a column that had no business existing. Just **instability** threaded between a transit lip where mouths moved and static arrived and a Relay spine where Dubai sold wonder while load oscillated in margins tourists never learned on purpose.

Mara's pen stopped above the notebook.

Pattern mind disease—the investigator reflex that found signal in noise and couldn't always tell whether that was virtue or appetite. **4w5** focus: store first, name later, shame at arriving late only after the file agreed to carry what your body already knew.

* * *

The comparison pane opened when she requested merge—not **8841-A** convergence, not yet, not the ninety-seven percent bar Month Five would eventually hold without drama. Partial. Deliberate withhold dressed as access control.

**CROSS-SITE CONTAMINATION INDEX · PRELIMINARY**

Fields she had seen in redacted margins for months without seeing **named** on a screen she was allowed to hold:

**Salt contamination — flagged.**

Not *positive*. Not *residue*. Not the four-letter obscenity laboratory font would later make small on purpose. **Flagged**—partial naming, worse than silence because silence let you pretend ignorance was structural. This was the institution admitting it had a noun and choosing not to finish the sentence in rooms that still called Still Ones evaluation holds.

Connection unclear.

Locked.

Dubai relay trace and Harbor sync anomaly and a third site code she didn't recognize at first glance—**G-009-ADJACENT · EDUCATIONAL BAND · SAMPLE WITHHELD**—suggested without proving, rhymed without merging, the way partial reports protected liability while still letting pattern minds bleed. The educational band note sat wrong in her chest—not because she knew what G-009 meant on-page, but because **withheld** beside **adjacent** was how institutions said *close enough to compare, far enough to deny.*

No proof chain complete. No mechanism rescue. No physics lecture about quantum disruption or embodiment layers or consciousness persistence probability. Just labels on gray UI and a crystallography abstract thumbnail that looked like geology pretending to be medicine—pretty lattice, meaningless caption, **almost** enough to make horror feel like data entry until your body refused the alibi.

She zoomed the thumbnail once. Salt geometry in false color. Scan match percentage blurred behind a redaction bar. **PRELIMINARY · NOT FOR FIELD RELEASE.** The screen offered beauty where the notebook wanted a noun finished.

Mara horror.

Body first. Throat tight. Stomach drop. Professional mask micro-crack—the same hairline fracture Month One had taught her in Hush Spire when eyes tracked with patience that felt personal. Not scream. Not meltdown. **4w5** swallow: witness work at institutional scale, horror honest and private in a room that did not sweeten its outputs.

She thought of static on a public lip without naming the week. She thought of a kitchen somewhere where a child had asked why officials looked scared while anchors said recovery. She thought of secret reviews that continued where continuing meant quietly. Different registers. Same family. Connection **unclear**—and that unclear worse than any single answer because unclear meant the institution knew enough to compare sites and not enough—or not willing—to tell civilians what comparison implied.

She did not write *they are awake* in the notebook.

That line lived in files she hadn't earned yet. This month only offered **Salt contamination — flagged** and a cross-ref table that refused to close.

* * *

The side channel opened the way side channels opened when bureaucracy forgot investigators were also witnesses—notification drawer, internal routing leak, the whisper family Ch 40 had taught her to capture before the cloud decided she hadn't meant it.

Not council scene. Not villain speech. Memo flash on a pane that wasn't supposed to mirror to her clearance tier:

**CONTINUITY INTERNAL · GUIDANCE REVIEW · HARBOR / DUBAI CROSS-SITE**  
**RECOMMENDATION: DO NOT RELEASE PUBLIC GUIDANCE.**  
**RATIONALE: CONTAINMENT OF UNNECESSARY ALARM · MARKET STABILITY · OPEN LANES.**  
**DISTRIBUTION: REVIEW CELL ONLY · NOT FOR PUBLIC CHANNELS**

Below the header, bullet rationales in the institution's calmest font—**avoid secondary panic**, **preserve transit confidence**, **defer cross-site narrative until market close**—each bullet a small murder dressed as prudence.

Do not release public guidance.

Verbatim. Locked. The memo contradicted every public footer Noah's neighbors were swallowing with dinner—**CONTINUITY SECURES YOUR EVENING · OPEN LANES · RECOVERY NOMINAL**—without requiring Mara to replay kitchens on-page. Cover-up grammar at classified scale. Denial pair. Guidance withheld while sync instability threaded between cities that performed recovery on schedule.

Mara captured the memo header—not synced, not admissible, private folder parallel to tier rollups and harbor frames and child art she had not filed. Phone camera off terminal glass because some evidence chains started where bureau policy ended and her father's ledger voice still whispered *numbers lie slower* when institutions moved faster than truth.

**Salt · flagged · connection unclear · guidance withheld.**

She saved it beside Harbor static and LATTICE micro-blink and the refusal logged in a wellness partition she could not unchoose. Not theology. **Pattern**—the investigator disease Lena had named and the queue kept rewarding. Evidence without authority. Horror without mechanism complete. Naming partial worse than silence.

The terminal fan whirred. The red band held. Outside the secure room, feeds would keep selling Dubai recovering and Harbor resolved and markets steady in footers most people wouldn't scroll to.

Mara closed the fragment viewer.

Her hands were steady enough for badge-out. Her chest was not. Trust frayed again—not dramatic, **hairline**—the same fracture Ch 44 had seeded in kitchens she hadn't entered and Ch 43 had seeded on a public lip and Month Three had seeded when prediction blinked without memo.

She did not ping Chen.

Not yet. Not with a partial noun and a cross-ref table and a memo line the institution would call containment if she called it cover-up. She had a folder on her phone growing like a private bureau the institution couldn't grade. She had **8841-A** waiting in a side tab for convergence she wasn't ready to file. She had Salt **flagged** without mechanism and connection unclear without mercy and public guidance explicitly withheld while cities kept smiling on schedule.

She badged out into a corridor that did not perform gold and did not apologize for what files had started admitting in fragments.

Dubai would still be selling wonder on feeds by morning. Harbor would still call the incident resolved. Markets would still tick green in corners Noah Vale might scroll past while muting reassurance.

Mara walked toward the elevator with Salt flagged and connection unclear and a memo line saved where the institution could not grade it.

Memo.

Guidance withheld.

Horror without the name complete.
