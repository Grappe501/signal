## Performance notes

**Vale:** Play **technically accurate · emotionally delayed** — not robotic, not villain. Dementia model: present but unreachable.

**Mara:** Grief before fear. Do not play horror reaction in mesh view. Human test: hope visible, then break.

**Dog:** One shot. Non-approach. No explanation VO.
