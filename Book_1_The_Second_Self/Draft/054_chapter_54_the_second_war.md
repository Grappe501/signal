The simulation suite did not perform awe.

Month Four, week nineteen. Greenland LATTICE annex—deeper in the stack than the model floor where quarterly reviews filed human volatility in clinical font, behind bands the public layer never learned to read. White light. Filtered air cold enough to feel like honesty. Not the Arctic Core pit investigators would eventually stand inside with witness tags and Relay hum beneath their boots. **Simulations** only—the grammar internal continuity enforced: seeds allowed, engagement forbidden on public channels.

She badged in with the unhurried competence of someone whose shift had always ended before the model failed. Routine posture sweep. Deployment calendar green in the corner where green still meant *continue*, not *safe*. The lead nodded once—not welcome, procedure.

"We're not expecting engagement," he said. "We're expecting **preparation**."

Preparation. Not war. The distinction mattered the way it mattered in rooms that processed weather for a world still pretending weather was local.

Two analysts at the side table did not look up from their keyboards. That was faith in architecture—heads down while the map promised continuity, the way district footers promised open lanes in fonts most people wouldn't scroll to. The hum under the floor did not rise to music. Relay frequency continued—the undertone of a latitude that had decided distance was rude and built cathedrals for machines to answer it.

* * *

The suite opened on **posture maps**—overlay stacks in gray UI the way classified fragments listed infrastructure failures in bullet grammar too dry to scream.

**CHIRUSSIA · STRATEGIC POSTURE · SIMULATION ONLY**  
**IRAN · REGIONAL POSTURE · SIMULATION ONLY**  
**WESTERN HEMISPHERE · DEFENSIVE OVERLAY · SIMULATION ONLY**

**Models**—bodies as statistics, corridors as compression bands, resources as lines the civilization stack already routed through LATTICE before presidents learned the nouns for briefings. ChiRussia sat on the left pane—not named as enemy, named as **posture**, alignment without spectacle, inevitability without parade. Iran ticked regional in amber bands the overlay preferred because amber made tension legible before tension became headlines. Western defensive overlay stacked last—shield geometry, intake lips, sync corridors marked closed in simulation the way real corridors had been marked closed in Month Two when bodies chose another path anyway. The lead pulled a comparison clip without commentary—heat remapped, compression bands tightening along a northern corridor the public map still labeled **OPEN**. Bodies in the model moved where the model permitted. Bodies in the archive clip moved where habit permitted. The mismatch did not alarm the suite. It **updated** the overlay and called the delta variance.

She thought of a civilization coordinates file logged in queues she didn't open—topology where governments funded and argued and issued statements while Tier-0 routing decided what must stay synchronized. Partial governors. Statement authority. Presidents in briefing nouns. Continuity directors in routing nouns. Here the military layer stacked beneath all of it: **preparing**, not **fighting**, the way weather stacked beneath sunshine in districts that still sold recovery in footers.

The lead ran a scenario without commentary—overlay refresh, sector heat remapped in colors the overlay preferred. A corridor emptied in simulation when routing marked closed and cooperation refused the node the model wanted. Cooperation without permission. The comparison pane from Month Four week sixteen rhymed: **HUMAN VOLATILITY CLUSTER · C-059 · UNMODELLED**. Faith. Rawist. Child behavior. Populations the civic map rendered null. Now **geopolitics** rendered the same way—gray absences where habit refused the node the model wanted.

A junior analyst cleared her throat and did not finish the sentence because finishing was what you did when the institution hadn't yet decided whether you were allowed to be surprised. The lead tapped **REFRESH** and said, "Expected variance. Within tolerance."

Within tolerance. The phrase sat wrong in her chest the way stale district tiles sat wrong in spectacle cities—lag at street level, gap at model level, confidence performing health while substrates kept private calendars.

Not war.

Preparing.

Her hands stayed still on the keyboard. Professional unease micro—not panic, not triumph, the body understanding before the memo did that systems prepared while civilians denied.

* * *

Resource fragility overlays next—**lines**. Sync dependency. Energy intake lips. Food routing bands. Minimal on purpose: the model didn't need to teach hunger to feel hunger when the graph showed compression crossing thresholds the public footer still called **RECOVERY NOMINAL**.

**SYNC DEPENDENCY · CROSS-BORDER · STRAINED**  
**ENERGY INTAKE · NORTHERN CORRIDOR · COMPRESSION RISING**  
**FOOD ROUTING · PRE-POSITION BANDS · BELOW NOMINAL BUFFER**

The overlays stacked like weather systems—each line thin, each line real, none of them asking permission to become crisis. A side monitor scrolled district recovery footers from elsewhere—**OPEN LANES · SPECTACLE UNINTERRUPTED · RECOVERY NOMINAL**—background wrong calm while the fragility pane kept listing dependencies the public layer treated as metaphors.

**LATTICE CONFIDENCE · AGGREGATE · 30-DAY ROLLUP**  
**PRIOR · 96.2%**  
**CURRENT · 71.8% · STRAINED**

**Low**—the way tier rollups had strained when micro-red ticks appeared mid-briefing and the UI patched faster than humans could articulate doubt. The same family as blind spots filed under **HUMAN VOLATILITY CLUSTER**—coordinates on one screen, confidence leaking on another while green icons continued their work in a corner of the room that wasn't supposed to be the point. Month Four week sixteen had named the human gap in clinical font. Month Four week nineteen showed the aggregate band **straining** under posture sweeps the public layer would never learn to read.

Instability curve.

The graph filled the lower pane—global, not heroic, **up** in the direction no one put in keynote slides. **GLOBAL INSTABILITY INDEX · CLASSIFIED · SIMULATION SUITE**—rising through Month Four on a slope the institution preferred to file where filing meant contained.

**0.39 · 0.44 · 0.51 · 0.58**

Resource fragility at civilization scale—dependency visible in curves before streets finished arguing about whether dependency was a metaphor. The lead did not propose ceasefire. The suite did not authorize manual override for geopolitics. The curve was **named** so bureaucracy could continue, not **closed** so civilization could breathe.

She zoomed one overlay once. Western defensive band. Iranian regional tick. ChiRussia strategic posture shifting a half-degree in simulation—the kind of half-degree that wouldn't make feeds and would make classified reports. Realignment seed, not live engagement. Curves rising while posture maps pretended to be neutral.

The lead watched the refresh complete and did not comment. Comment was what you offered when the institution still believed surprise could be scheduled.

The lead closed the overlay stack without ceremony. "Posture complete. Fragility confirmed. File as scheduled." Scheduled meant classified. Scheduled meant contained. Scheduled meant the world above could keep performing peace while the stack below kept performing preparation.

* * *

The filing terminal sat at the end of the shift the way verdicts sat at the end of trials—clean UI, heavy consequence, send button dressed as procedure.

**GLOBAL INSTABILITY · INCREASING · SIMULATION CONFIRMED**  
**POSTURE SWEEPS · COMPLETE · NOT FOR PUBLIC CHANNELS**  
**B2 REALIGNMENT · SEED ONLY · PUBLIC ILLUSION LOCK · SIM ONLY**

Report classified.

Not villain speech. Not LATTICE anthropomorphized into an enemy you could refuse. Just a **label** for a slope the system admitted in clinical font because clinical font was how institutions made devastation administrable.

She read the draft once—not to fix it. There was no fix on offer. The review did not authorize manual override for human behavior or human politics. The instability was **filed** so procedure could survive the discovery the way procedure always survived discovery—by filing it where filing meant contained. Global instability **increasing** sat in the summary line the way *Do not release public guidance* had sat in other memos she had heard about secondhand—truth routed to internal channels while external channels kept their smile.

Submit chime—polite, satisfied, the acoustic equivalent of *within tolerance*. **SEND · CONFIRMED · CLASSIFIED ARCHIVE · CONTINUITY INTERNAL**. Distribution list blinked once—**REVIEW CELL · POSTURE ARCHIVE · CONTINUITY INTERNAL**—and closed. Bureaucracy continues. Shift ends. Procedure survives.

The team dispersed without alarm—badges chirping, headsets murmuring acronyms, the unhurried certainty of people who knew the building would still be standing when their shifts ended because standing was the building's job. Unease private. Not broadcast. Not kitchen scale. **Institutional** scale—filed where filing meant continue.

She saved local notes beside the submission hash—not synced, not testimony, witness habit seeding in a body trained to treat models as weather until weather stopped matching the window.

Preparing.

Not fighting.

Curve up.

The suite fans whirred. The posture maps refreshed green at the edges where green still meant continue. Outside this white room, districts still performed recovery and markets still ticked nominal and the show continued because the show was the product and the product still believed confidence bands could rise again if you reclassified the slope as variance before the next quarterly read.

She badged out into a corridor where the light stayed white and the air stayed cold and the hum beneath the floor continued.
