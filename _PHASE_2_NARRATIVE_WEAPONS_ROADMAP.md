# THE TEN NARRATIVE WEAPONS

## 1. THE CHARACTER DOSSIERS

### Most important remaining item

**Have:** Relationship webs (Seg 031), lusts (Seg 024), POV roles (Seg 034).

**Need (near-casting-sheet level) for:**

- **Mara**
- **Noah**
- **Mercer**
- **Lena**
- **Cass**
- **Vale**

**Missing per character:**

- exact ages
- physical appearance
- voice patterns
- flaws, fears, habits
- childhood wounds, secrets, contradictions

**Example gap (Mara):** We know she wants authenticity — but not what music she listens to, how she drinks coffee, siblings, regrets, why she became an investigator.

**Principle:** Audience forgives imperfect geopolitics/tech/economics. **Never forgives forgetable characters.**

## 3. THE RULES OF LATTICE

**Biggest worldbuilding hole remaining.**

**Have:** What it does, where it is, what it becomes (Seg 007, 023).

**Need:**

- What can LATTICE actually **see**?
- What **can't** it see?
- Can it **lie**? Make **mistakes**?
- Does it have **goals**?
- Who **audits** it?
- How does it **learn**?
- What **scares** LATTICE?

**Principle:** The smartest villain may be **a system.**

`[Integrated Seg 039: World_Bible/039_lattice_bible.md]`

## 4. THE HVI SYSTEM

- loopholes, black markets, corruption
